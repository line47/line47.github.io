I'm a front end developer and designer, with over 15 years of experience. I enjoy dabbling in engineering and working in government technology. Currently, I work for Ad Hoc where I focus on front end, user experience and web standards.

I've worked with various levels of federal and local government. My work includes National Science Foundation (NSF), the Thrift Savings Program (TSP), and Kentucky.gov. I am alum of both 18F and NIC Inc. and bring a passion for web standards, human centered design, and research.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Telecommunications from The University of Kentucky and live in Louisville, KY.
