Hey I'm Scott, a user expereince designer and front-end developer with over 15 years of experience in government technology. I enjoy making complex processes easier and more inclusive, building in the open and user research. Currently, I work for Ad Hoc as a Senior UX Deisigner : Front-end Specialist.

I've worked with various federal, state, and local governments. My work includes Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS), National Science Foundation (NSF), the Thrift Savings Program (TSP), and Kentucky.gov. I am alum of both 18F and NIC Inc. and bring a passion for web standards, human centered design, and research.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Telecommunications from The University of Kentucky and live in Asheville, NC. When I'm not working I like to compose and write music, spend time outdoors with my family, work in my garden, build things with my hands or enjoy a nice beer or bourbon.
